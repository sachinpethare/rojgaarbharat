Basic Typing Project

  • Our typing project work is the process of typing some documents by using MS-Word.
  • In basic typing project, you will earn up to 2/- rupees per document (We only pay for right entry).
  • The minimum basic payout for basic typing project is Rs.3000/-.
  • The payment cycle for this project is monthly, You will get all your earning monthly (From the date you have started your work).
  • You will receive your payout directly to your bank account or UPI whichever is convenient for you.
  • In this project, you will get total 1800 Docs assignment per month i.e daily you will get 60 document.
  • In basic typing project, you can earn maximum ₹3,000/- per month.
  • There is free registration but to start your work you have to pay for the basic typing project charges.
  • Validity of the project is 1 month. If you want to continue your work than just renew or upgrade your plan.
  • We will provide you scanned typing documents daily, You just have to type the document on MS-Word and you will earn Rs. 2/- for each right entry.
  • If you want to see the demo then click - View Demo
  • You have to complete all your assignment to request for a payout.
