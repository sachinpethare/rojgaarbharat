Activate Your Plan By Paying Activation Fees.

  1. Open any UPI App (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm etc.) on your phone.
  2. Tap on QR-Scanner on your UPI App and scan the QR-Code.
  3. Activate your plan by paying activation charges of your selected plan.
  4. Enter your valid E-mail ID and Phone No. in the "add your massage" section.

Note: PhonePe is not working currently due to some technical error please scan the QR code with Paytm or Google Pay and pay the activation charges.

Your plan will be activated within 24 hours and we will provide you the data once your plan is activated. If you have any doubts please feel free message us on WhatsApp.
